Discover Miramichi

This website was created as a project to help tourists and exchange students explore the city. Here you can discover restaurants, hotels, tourist attractions, activities, and places where you can relax, take photos, and enjoy the local culture.

Ritchie Wharf Park

is one of the most popular attractions in Miramichi. The waterfront boardwalk, playground, and river views make it a perfect place for photos and relaxing walks.

Tim Hortons

Tim Hortons is the most popular coffee spot in Canada. In Miramichi it works as a social hub where locals gather for coffee, donuts and conversation.

Saint Michael the Archangel Church

Centennial Bridge

A steel through-arch bridge over the Miramichi River, opened in 1967. It’s 1.1 km long, 73 m high, connecting Douglastown and Chatham, replacing the old Romeo & Juliet ferry.

Napan Baykery

A family-owned bakery in Miramichi offering European and German-style breads and pastries, located at 1802 Water St, famous for fresh goods and a cozy atmosphere.
